ID Fraud

Identity fraud is a crime where one person uses another person's personal data, without
authorization, to deceive or defraud that person.

The fraudster then uses this personal information to, for example, open a credit card
account without permission, and then charge merchandise to that account.

Identity fraud does not occur when a credit card is simply stolen; that may be consumer fraud,
but is not identity fraud.

Identity fraud is a synonym of unlawful identity change.

It indicates unlawful activities that use the identity of another person or of a non-existing
person fraudulently.
